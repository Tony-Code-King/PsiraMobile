What is an MVNO?

An MVNO (Mobile Virtual Network Operator) is a company that provides mobile services but does not own the physical network infrastructure. MVNOs lease network capacity from traditional mobile network operators and offer their services under their own brand.

How do I get a Psira SIM card?

You can purchase a Psira SIM card from our website, authorized dealers, or retail stores. Once you have the SIM card, you can activate it by following the instructions provided or through our mobile app.

What are the benefits of using Psira's mobile internet service?

Psira offers affordable and reliable mobile internet plans with flexible data bundles. Our network coverage is extensive, and we provide high-speed data connectivity across South Africa.

How does Psira's mobile wallet work?

Psira's mobile wallet is a secure digital wallet that allows you to store and transfer funds, pay bills, and make purchases using your mobile device. It's a convenient and cashless way to manage your finances on the go.

Can I use my Psira SIM card and mobile wallet internationally?

Yes, you can use your Psira SIM card and mobile wallet in countries where we have roaming agreements. However, international roaming charges may apply, and certain mobile wallet features may be limited in some countries.

How do I check my Psira account balance and usage?

You can check your account balance, data usage, and other details through our mobile app, by dialing a USSD code, or by logging into your online account on our website.

What should I do if I lose my Psira SIM card or mobile device?

If you lose your Psira SIM card or mobile device, contact our customer support immediately. We can block your SIM card and mobile wallet to prevent unauthorized access and assist you with obtaining a replacement.
